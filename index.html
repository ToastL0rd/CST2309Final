Nerds and Jocks Unite!

Powerup Gym is a gymnasium and electronic repair hybrid business. We offer various events related to exercise, and in electronics.

Our Gym (ground floor) offers copious space for weight lifting, treadmills, ellipticals, and a dedicated mat area for crossfit, yoga, and rope climbing. We also provide classes for crossfit, small scale cycling classes, and even weight lifting tournaments.

The Electronics Repair portion of Powerup Gym provides services for PC, Laptop, and Smartphone repair. Our employees also take part in educational classes to teach techniques such as device troubleshooting, soldering tips, and electronic fundamental tutoring. Electronic repair services can be found on the 2nd floor of the building

Why a Gym AND Electronic Repair?

Because no one else has done it before! We feel that providing a space for the physically fit, with the technically inclined creates an environment that is beneficial to both sides. Those who are in peak physique can find a space to have their wireless headphones repaired, or their laptops refurbished.
