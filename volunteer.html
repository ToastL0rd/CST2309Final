Want to help Power Up Gym?

Power Up gym relies on the community for both faculty and income. Our electronic repair shop has both
volunteers, and paid employees to ensure both aspects of our mission is fufilled. While our paid employees
ensures our establishment run in top shape, we also rely on volunteers to assist when possible.Those who
want to volunteer have a range of options available to them. For example, the simplest way you can help
is by donating components. These could be from providing components like resistors, soldering tips, flux
or even donating computer components or LCD panels. We also accept those willing to donate their time to
lend their skills to the repair shop by assisting in repairs. Those who do spend their free time in our
electronic shop can expect to learn how to make basic repairs to phones, laptops, PCs, and even our own
gym equipment. Any volunteering work involving physical attendance that results in over 5 hours of work
will be guarenteed a reference at any job application if needed. Simply choose a date, and we will inform
you of opening times on that day via email. We only allow five volunteers per day, so act fast if you
want a spot!
